The advantage of using JPG compression is that it allows you to reduce the image file size. This comes in handy if you want to upload images to the internet or send them via e-mail.

JPG compression is usually lossy, which means that some of the visual information is getting lost. This may affect the image quality.

How to reduce image file size?

  1. Upload your image file, may it be a JPG, PNG, TIFF or camera RAW image.
  2. Choose a compression level from the dropdown menu.
  3. Toggle the image quality to optimize your result. (optional)
  4. Click on "Start" to start the compression process.
